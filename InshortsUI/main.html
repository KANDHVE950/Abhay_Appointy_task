Jio fastest mobile network with 19.3 Mbps average download speed

Reliance Jio continues to remain the fastest mobile network with an average download speed of 19.3 Mbps in September, according to TRAI data. Idea Cellular network (now Vodafone Idea) followed Jio with a download speed of 8.6 Mbps, Vodafone 7.9 Mbps and Bharti Airtel 7.5 Mbps. Though Vodafone and Idea have merged their mobile businesses, TRAI measured their performance separately.

OnePlus 8T 5G set to launch globally on October 14

OnePlus will launch the OnePlus 8T 5G globally and in India at 7:30 pm IST via live stream on the OnePlus YouTube channel and also through the OnePlus World experience. The OnePlus World is a 360-degree interactive virtual world offering various activities. The smartphone is said to be powered with Android 11 with a 120Hz refresh rate AMOLED display.

Man who wanted to visit Mecca cheated of ₹12.26L by travel agent in Mumbai

A 62-year-old man was cheated of ₹12.26 lakh by a travel agent in Mumbai, who took the money to arrange a trip for him and his family to Mecca and Medina, the police said. The accused, who was a friend of the complainant, kept postponing the trip and later cancelled it, police added. He is yet to return the money.
